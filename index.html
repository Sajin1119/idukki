About Us

WHO ARE WE?

When we moved to Canada we were all excited to try the multitude of cuisines that we knew would be available here…. but one thing we didn’t expect is that too much was missing ,the very taste of home. Most of us were lucky and lets say we took it for granted having 3 tasty meals a day.! And then ..we landed in the land of mighty Goose !! From enjoying a junior chicken and sipping on an iced-capp to a meal a day and hungry nights we have been through it all . And now, we miss the Appam and stew on Sunday mornings . Dosa and Omelette from our favourite late night thattukada , The Sharjah shake that gave us brain freeze . Chicken fry with that extra podi and even the dosa amma played on repeat. This feeling is what lead to the beginning of IDUKKI GOLD . We wanted to bring you a place where you can have a meal that tastes like home . We want to create a hangout spot like many we had back home – where you get to make some amazing memories along with heart warming food. We have been working and planning on this moment and finally we are so excited to let you guys know – We are here Team IDUKKI GOLD
